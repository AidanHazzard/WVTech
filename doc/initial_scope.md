
This app is designed to help user plan their meals, manage groceries, and help stay on track with their nutrition along with time and budget goals. Users will create an account and from their add meals and recipes to a calendar. They can also choose to repeat certain meals across the days of their choosing, and automatically generate a shopping list based on these planned meals. This system can also help reduce any stress by being able to recommend recipes that fit any restictions the user may have. (i.e. allergies, bugdet limits, dietary restrictions, and prep time.) While keeping in consideration the ingredients that is more frequently used by the user. By combining meal planning, grocery tracking, recipes suggestions and nutrion summaries in one app, it can help solve problems such as forgetting ingredients, overspending on grociers and consistently planning meal all-together.
